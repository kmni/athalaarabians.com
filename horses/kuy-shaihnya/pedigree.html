Kuy Shaihnya

KUY SHAIHNYA is a proven show mare, having won her World Class at the 2000 Egyptian Event. She is also a proven producer as three of her offspring have won halter titles at the Egyptian Event. Her son SHARIF EMIR PG is a Top Ten Futurity Colt. Her daughter SAHARA PG is a Top Ten Yearling and Two Year Old Futurity Filly and was exported to Israel, as was her full sister SHARJAH PG, an Egyptian Breeders Challenge Champion Filly and Top Ten Yearling Futurity Filly. KUY SHAIHNYAs daughter SHAHEERAH PG was exported to Kuwait.

KUY SHAIHNYA is a full sister to KUY MAYA, dam of Egyptian Event Top Ten Yearling Futurity Colt MAJEED SUHAIL PG (exported to Belgium) and of 5 times Egyptian Event halter winner MALAWI PG, whose titles include a World Class Top Ten and two Futurity Top Ten titles. KUY SHAIHNYA is also a 3/4 sister to KUY ALI AMIRAH, dam of Egyptian Event Top Ten World Class Yearling Colt KUY ALYKHAN.

KUY SHAIHNYA represents the best of the breeding programs of the two most significant American Egyptian breeding programs: Gleannloch and Ansata. She is a direct granddaughter of the National Champions SHAIKH AL BADI and NABIEL+/, both considered among the best Egyptian Arabians bred in America. She is also double *MAGIDAA. *MAGIDAA was one of the most significant mares imported to America, and produced 4 champions, 3 National winners and 2 producers of National winners.

KUY SHAIHNYA is a daughter of Region 16 Top Five Stallion AK SHAIKH, who sired just 7 registered foals. All of his producing daughters have become dams of Egyptian Event winners. AK SHAIKH is a 3/4 brother to U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion and National Champion Futurity Colt RUMINAJA ALI, as he is by SHAIKH AL BADI out of a *MAGIDAA daughter. RUMINAJA ALI sired 11 National winners and 19 producers of National winners, including U.S. and Canadian National Champion Stallion ALI JAMAAL (an international sire of National Champions), U.S. and Canadian Top Ten Stallion THE MINSTRIL(sire of U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion THEE DESPERADO, the leading living Egyptian sire of champions), U.S. Top Ten Stallion ANAZA EL FARID (sire of U.S. Reserve National Champion Senior Stallion and World Champion Stallion *GAZAL AL SHAQAB), U.S. National Champion Native Costume and National Champion sire FOCUS ALIMUS+/, and U.S. Top Ten Show Hack and National Champion sire FOCUS FLAME.

AK SHAIKH is by U.S. Reserve National Champion Futurity Stallion SHAIKH AL BADI, sire of 14 National winners including U.S. and Canadian National Champion Western Pleasure AOTR OUR CLEOPATRA. SHAIKH AL BADI is by *MORAFIC out of U.S. Top Ten Mare, English Pleasure and Park *BINT MAISA EL SAGHIRA++, dam of 5 champions and 3 producers of National winners.

AK SHAIKH is out of AK KANTAARA, whose daughter ASAIAHS KAHLAARA produced multi-halter champion JUDAHS NEFERTITI. AK KANTAARAs granddaughter MINI KANTAARA was exported to Israel, while another granddaughter MB KATEENA was exported to Bahrain. MB KATEENA was a 2005 Egyptian Event Leading Halter Dam and produced 2004 Egyptian Breeders Challenge Champion Filly KATARINAH KA and 2005 Champion Yearling Futurity Colt MAARIQ KA.

AK KANTAARA is by U.S Top Ten Stallion *IBN MONIET EL NEFOUS, sire of 11 National winners in halter and performance including U.S. National Champion Western Pleasure NAZMON CW and Canadian Reserve National Champion Pleasure Driving PRINCESS OF EGYPT++. 12 *IBN MONIET EL NEFOUS daughters have produced National winners and their offspring include National Champions in hunt pleasure, show hack and halter as well as World Reserve Champion Mare IMPERIAL PHANILAH. Her dam *MAGIDAA produced 5 champions including U.S. National Champion Futurity Colt and Top Ten Stallion NABIEL+/ (maternal grandsire of KUY SHAIHNYA), U.S. National Champion Futurity Filly SHAFEEKAH, and Canadian Top Ten English Pleasure and Pleasure Driving SUGAA+//. *MAGIDAAs daughter, halter reserve champion BINT MAGIDAA, is the dam of 7 champions including U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion RUMINAJA ALI, U.S. Top Ten Stallion and National winner sire THEE INFIDEL, and U.S. Top Ten Futurity Colt ALIDAAR (also a multi-champion in Europe and the Middle East and a sire of National winners worldwide).

KUY SHAIHNYA is out of DB NASEEMA, whose two producing daughters are both dams of Egyptian Event winners. DB NASEEMA is a full blood sister to U.S. Top Ten Futurity Filly ANSATA SAROYA, who is by her sire NABIEL+/ out of a full sister to her dam. She is a paternal sister to 15 National winners including U.S. Reserve National Champion Mare AOTH RA TIMRA+, multi-National Champion Dressage AF THE MAGICIAN+/, World Reserve Champion Colt SABIELL and Middle East Champion Stallion ROYAL MIKHIEL. DB NASEEMA is by U.S. National Champion Futurity Colt and Top Ten Stallion NABIEL+/, whose get have produced National Champions in halter, western pleasure, hunt over fences and jumping. NABIEL+/ is a full brother to National winner in English pleasure and pleasure driving SUGAA+// His sire *SAKR++ won 14 National titles in halter and performance and sired National Champions in halter and performance. NABIEL+/s dam is the exceptional producer *MAGIDAA, already discussed.

DB NASEEMA is out of ANSATA SALOME, whose daughters have been exported to England and Germany. ANSATA SALOME is a full sister to National winner producer ANSATA SAMIRA, dam of U.S. Top Ten Futurity Filly and Egyptian Event halter winner producer ANSATA SAROYA. She is a 3/4 sister to ANSATA WANISA, dam of Egyptian Event Reserve Champion Yearling Futurity Filly DAFIINAHH. She is by three times U.S. Top Ten Stallion *ANSATA IBN HALIMA++, sire of 14 North American National winners. He is the only imported Egyptian stallion to sire both a U.S. National Champion Stallion (ANSATA IBN SUDAN) and a U.S. National Champion Mare (FA HALIMA). His son ANSATA HALIM SHAH, a U.S. Top Ten Futurity Colt and World Reserve Junior Champion Colt, is the sire of National Champions in the Middle East and Europe including World Champion Stallion *AL ADEED AL SHAQAB. *ANSATA IBN HALIMA++ is also the sire of U.S. and Canadian Top Ten Stallion EL HILAL, the all time leading Egyptian sire of National winners. ANSATA SALOMEs dam *ANSATA BINTNAZEER is a full sister to *BINT EL BATAA, dam of SANKT GEORG RSI (maternal grandsire of U.S. National Champion Stallion and World Champion Stallion *SIMEON SHAI+) and of National Champion sire SHIKO IBN SHEIKH (paternal grandsire of multi-National Champion Park AEQUUS+//).
