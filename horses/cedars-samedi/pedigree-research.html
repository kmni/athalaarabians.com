CEDARS SAMEDI

 

CEDARS SAMEDI is a paternal sister to UK Egyptian Event Supreme Champion Mare SHARE THE JOY, to U.A.E. Champion Mare and Al Khalediah Reserve Senior Champion Mare MMECCA, as well as to BARAKIS WHISPER, dam of twice Israeli National Champion Colt MOUADAAR BKV, a sire of Israeli National winners in 2010. CEDARS SAMEDI is a full sister to junior champion colt CEDARS BARAKE, who was exported to Mexico. CEDARS SAMEDI has two lines to U.S. Top Ten Stallion *JAMILLL, also sire of World Champion Mare TIFFAHA and of IMPERIAL MISTILLL, the all time leading Egyptian dam of champions with 9 champions and 3 National winners


CEDARS SAMEDI is by the unshown black stallion AL BARAKI,? who in addition to his international winners,has sired 17 American Egyptian Event winners. He was ranked as a Leading Halter Sire at the Event for 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2008 and 2009 and as a Leading Halter and Performance Sire in 2005. AL BARAKI?s winning get at the Egyptian Event include Reserve Champion Stallion AOTH SAQQARA and 2010 First Place World Class Mares 8-9 EVANGELINE CC.?


AL BARAKI is a full brother to U.S. Top Ten Futurity Colt SHABAB JAMIL and is a 3/4 brother to halter champion and Egyptian Event halter winner THE ATTICUS (sire of Canadian Top Ten Futurity Colt MB SCATMAN and 13 Egyptian Event halter winners including the Reserve Junior Champions MB SHATTAL and MB MYSTICAL).?

AL BARAKI is by U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion THEE DESPERADO, sire of 12 North American National winners through 2010. They include Canadian Reserve National Champion Stallion AOTH THEE GAMBLER, U.S. Top Ten Futurity Colt and Stallion and National winner sire THEE INFIDEL, U.S. National Champion Mare AOTH PR DESERT ROSE+, U.S. Reserve National Champion Jumper ATR SA THEMOONDANCER, and U.S. Reserve National Champion Mare JTH LF TRIUMPHANT STAR. THEE DESPERADO also sired 1998 European ?Triple Crown? winner BJ THEE MUSTAFA, named Nations Cup and European and World Champion Stallion, twice Israeli National Champion Mare and National Champion producer THE VISION HG,? THEE RICOCHET(sire of 2004 Egyptian International Reserve Junior Champion Filly MOUTAZAT AL BIWIBIYA and 2006 Egyptian International Reserve Junior Champion Colt WARAD AL KHALEDIAH), 2004 Israeli Reserve National Champion Stallion ENTEBBE CA, three times South African Champion Stallion THEE CYCLONE, British National Champion Colt THEE EQUALIZER, 2009 Egyptian National Bronze Champion Colt MAZYOUN EL SHERUK, 2006 Jordanian National Champion Colt and 2010 Middle East Bronze Champion Stallion TAJ TAI and 2010 Israeli Silver Champion Stallion TRUE COLOURS.


THEE DESPERADO sons who have sired National winners include BB THE RENEGADE (sire of U.S. Top Ten Hunt Pleasure Futurity and Canadian Top Ten Hunt Pleasure JTR 14-17 and Hunt Pleasure Select ATR GALI GEE WHIZ SMF and paternal grandsire of U.S. National Champion Mare AOTH OM EL SHADREAM),? BOTSWANA (sire of 2007 Israeli Egyptian Event Reserve Champion Mare KENYA PG), THEE INFIDEL (sire of 2 Sport Horse National halter winners), THEE VERDICT HG (sire of 2007 Scandinavian Reserve Champion Colt FLM VERDI) and TRUE COLOURS (sire of World? Champion Stallion ROYAL COLOURS).


AL BARAKI is out of National winner producer ALI JAMILA, dam of 4 Egyptian Event halter winners. ALI JAMILA was exported to Kuwait in 2000. Besides THE ATTICUS and SHABAB JAMIL, they are ANJAMILA (3 titles) and BINT BINT JUSTINA (6 titles), both full siblings to AL BARAKI. ?BINT BINT JUSTINA is the dam of 2008 Egyptian Event Junior Champion Colt JUSTYNN and 2010 Egyptian Breeders Challenge Reserve Champion Filly JUSTYNNA. Six ALI JAMILA offspring have produced Egyptian Event winners.

ALI JAMILA is by U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion RUMINAJA ALI, sire of 11 National winners and 19 producers of National winners, including U.S. and Canadian National Champion Stallion ALI JAMAAL (an international sire of National Champions), U.S. and Canadian Top Ten Stallion THE MINSTRIL (sire of U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion THEE DESPERADO, the leading living Egyptian sire of champions), U.S. Top Ten Stallion ANAZA EL FARID (sire of U.S. Reserve National Champion Senior Stallion and World Champion Stallion *GAZAL AL SHAQAB), U.S. National Champion Native Costume and National Champion sire FOCUS ALIMUS+/, and U.S. Top Ten Show Hack and National Champion sire FOCUS FLAME.

ALIJAMILA is out of ANSATA JUSTINA, dam of 5 Egyptian Event halter winners: SKYY (4 titles), JUBILLLEE (2 titles), THEE MAGI, THEPRIME MINSTRIL and THEE BRIGADIER (the latter also German Junior Champion Colt and Wels International Champion Colt and a sire of National Champions in Europe).? ANSATA JUSTINA?s daughter JUBILLLEE is the dam of 5 Egyptian Event halter winners CN JERICHO (also an Egyptian Event performance winner and Leading Halter and Performance sire), THEE CAPPUCCINO, DESHA JAVA., and Reserve Supreme Champion Stallion and Leading Halter Sire INSIGNIA DE SHA (sire of 17 Egyptian Event halter winners including Junior Champion Fillies INSIGNIAS SPIRIT and SAREAH SAKKARA and, Supreme Champion Gelding MOSI). ANSATA JUSTINA is closely related to leading Egyptian dam of champons IMPERIAL MISTILLL as both are by *JAMILLL and ANSATA JUSTINA is out of an *ANSATA IBN HALIMA++ daughter while IMPERIAL MISTILLL is out of a double granddaughter of *ANSATA IBN HALIMA++.

CEDARS SAMEDI is out of SHALOM SABBATH, who produced just 4 registered foals before her early death at age 10. Two are champions: CEDARS BARAKE and reserve junior halter champion and Egyptian Event Top Ten World Class Filly CEDARS KASANE. SHALOM SABBATH comes from a very athletic line of horses.

SHALOM SABBATH?s sire *IBN AMOURA sired multi-Canadian National Champion Dressage SHALOM YAROM+/, champion hunter over fences SHALOM AMNON,? Region 18 Reserve Champion Jumper SHALOM TIKVAH, and Region 18 Champion Second Level Dressage SHALOM MA-AJAN, and the successful endurance horse EL ALARIB, who has 1500 ride miles to his credit.? *IBN AMOURA is by IBN ABLA, a paternal brother to the National winner producers *SANAAA (dam of 3 National winners in halter, two of them National Champion sires), *BINT BINT EL SAMRAA (dam of multi-National Champion Western Pleasure ALMILEEGY++ and multi-National winner in western pleasure *IBN AL HASSAN+/), FARASHA (dam of multi-National Champion Native Costume and Sidesaddle and National winner sire? *FALEH++) and *BERLANTY (dam of U.S. Top Ten Mare and National winner producer *SHIAA). *IBN AMOURA?s dam? AMOURA I is a paternal sister to *GAZBEYA (dam of multi-National Champion Trail EL BARRAKA+) NADEEMAH (dam of National winner sire FIKRI) and to *BAHEIA, third dam of U.S. and Canadian Top Ten Stallion and National Champion sire THE MINSTRIL.


SHALOM SABBATH is out of SHALOM JAMILIA. She is by *NORUS, sire of Canadian Top Ten Stallion, Egyptian Event Supreme Champion Stallion and World Champion Stallion? and National Champion sire HADIDI. *NORUS is a 3/4 brother to SCHARAYA, dam of Scottsdale Champion Stallion *SHAREM EL SHEIKH (sire of U.S. and Canadian National Champion Stallion ETERNETY+).


SHALOM JAMILIA is out of *BINT BINT JAMIL, dam of PIMLICO RCA, the 2004 Egyptian Event Supreme Champion Stallion and 2007 Region 9 Champion Stallion. He was also 2008 Arabian Breeders World Cup Top Five Stallion 11 and Over. He has sired15 Egyptian Event winners and was named a Leading Halter Sire at the Egyptian Event in 2003, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009 and 2010.? *BINT BINT JAMIL is also the dam of halter champion and 2005 Egyptian Event Second Place Extended Specialty Mare ASMARI JAMILA DMF.? *BINT BINT JAMIL is by MOHAFEZ, who was bred in the US (where he was named AK SHARAF) and exported to Germany, where he sired 1984 German Reserve National Champion Stallion IBN MOHAFEZ. MOHAFEZ is a full blood brother to Canadian Top Ten Stallion NA IBN MONIET, maternal grandsire of twice World Reserve Champion Stallion and British National Champion Stallion CRUSADER and Swedish Reserve National Champion Stallion SHAHIN. *BINT BINT JAMIL is out of *SEHNAB, dam of 2010 Egyptian Event Leading Halter Sire MB IBN SEHNAB. She is by U.S. Top Ten Stallion *JAMILLL. *SEHNAB?s dam SABAH is a paternal sister to National winner sire IBN GALAL I (sire of U.S. Top Ten Mare *IBN GALAL 1-7) and to Australian National Champion Mare 27 IBN GALAL 5, the maternal granddam of U.S. and Canadian National Champion Stallion and World Champion Stallion *SIMEON SHAI+.
