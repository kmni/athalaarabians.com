A Canen's Pedigree'

A CANEN has done well in the show ring on the regional level, winning as a yearling and a two year old (he was named Region 2 Reserve Champion Two Year Old Colt) and also being named a 2010 Las Vegas World Cup Top Five Two Year Old Colt.? A CANEN is a paternal brother to National Champions in Europe, the Middle East and North America and he comes from a dam line that has produced National winners for four generations. He is linebred to U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion RUMINAJA ALI, to Canadian Reserve National Champion Park GDANSK+/, and to U.S. National Champion Stallion and Park *BASK++, to whom he has four lines. He is very smooth bodied, with a lovely shape to his neck, huge eyes, and has a wonderful show attitude. He offers an ideal outcross for MAGNUM PSYCHE or *MARWAN AL SHAQAB daughters.

A CANEN is by U.S. and Canadian Reserve National Champion Futurity Colt and U.S. and Canadian Top Ten Junior Stallion *BESSON CAROL, sire of 49 champions and 13 National winners. His get include the National Champions in halter FIORELLO, MAJESSTIC, and DC BENEDICT (the latter also twice a National winner in hunt pleasure) as well as the National Champions in western pleasure RESSTITUTION, DESTINATIONN and MEGGA STAR+/( the latter a four time National Champion in western pleasure). His son FADI AL SHAQAB has been named Gold Champion Colt at both the 2010 Al Khalediah Show in Saudi Arabia and the Mediterranean Championships in Menton, France and World Silver Champion Colt in Paris. Another son, FOREVER BESSON, was named Champion Stallion at the 2008 Casino Cup Show in Austria. His son ANDREO is the 2010 Middle Eastern Silver Champion Colt. *BESSON CAROL get are also producing National Champions. His grandson SAN CARLOS JULLYEN V is a U.S. National Champion Hunt Pleasure Futurity winner. His granddaughter MADEIRA EL MARWAN is a 2010 Canadian Top Ten Two Year Old Filly. His grandson DIRAR AL SHAQAB is a Qatar National Champion Colt. He is a full brother to BAAHIR EL MARWAN, is a U.S. Top Ten Sweepstakes Colt and Scottsdale Junior Champion Colt BAAHIR EL MARWAN and to BAANDEROS is the 2010 World Champion Stallion BAANDEROS, also a World Junior Champion Colt as well as Champion Stallion at the Qatar National Show, Al Khalediah Show, and the 2010 Mediterranean Championship show.

*BESSON CAROL is by U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion *PARYS EL JAMAAL, sire of 8 National winners in halter and performance including the National Halter Champions *PETRUS CAROL, THE MILLENNIUM, SHALINA EL JAMAAL, and SERENATA EL JAMAAL. *PARYS EL JAMAAL get have produced multi-National Champion Halter MARHAABAH, National Champions MI MORENA and TRUE DESIRE LL, HARLEKYNE (dam of National Champion producer *PENNY CAROL) and ALFABIA DAMASCUS (sire of 2010 U.S. National Champion Junior Mare *ABHA QALAMS). *PARYS EL JAMAAL is a full brother to Argentine and Uruguayan Reserve National Champion Mare PERSJA EL JAMAAL. He is by U.S. and Canadian National Champion Stallion ALI JAMAAL, whose 29 North American National winners include U.S. National Champion Stallion *DAKAR EL JAMAAL and U.S. National Champion Mare *EUROPA EL JAMAAL. *PARYS EL JAMAAL?s dam is a daughter of Canadian Reserve National Champion Park and National Champion sire GDANSK+ and a granddaughter of U.S. National Champion Stallion? and Pleasure Driving and National Champion sire GAI PARADA+++/.

*BESSON CAROL is out of CLASSIC KRYSTALL, dam of regional reserve champion mare *PRIMROSE CAROL, dam of the National winners in halter MAGNUM PRIMIER and ADVANTUS PSY+.. CLASSIC KRYSTALL combines Russian, Polish and American blood. Her sire *SIMEON SHOR is a halter, western pleasure and hunt pleasure champion who has sired regional champions in hunt pleasure and dressage. He is by Dutch National Champion Stallion ANTEY and is a double grandget of National Champion sire ASWAN (as is the second dam of U.S. Reserve National Champion Stallion PADRONS PSYCHE). CLASSIC KRYSTALL is out of FRANGELICA, a daughter of U.S. National Champion Stallion *ALADDINN. Her dam ANNISETTE is by Canadian National Champion Park and National Champion sire COGNAC, a full brother to GDANSK+.

A CANEN is out of 2001 Egyptian Event Top Ten World Class Two Year Old Filly CANETTE, a full sister to halter AOTR reserve champion, hunt and western pleasure ribbon winner and four times Egyptian Event Top Ten Halter ROLEEYAH BEY.

CANNETTE's sire RAISIN THEE CANE is a regional, Scottsdale and Egyptian Event halter winner. From a very limited number of get due to his early death he has sired class A halter champions and Egyptian Event winners. RAISIN THEE CANE is by the leading living Egyptian sire of champions, THEE DESPERADO, who has sired 12 North American National winners through 2010. They include Canadian Reserve National Champion Stallion AOTH THEE GAMBLER, U.S. Top Ten Futurity Colt and Stallion and National winner sire THEE INFIDEL, U.S. National Champion Mare AOTH PR DESERT ROSE+, U.S. Reserve National Champion Jumper ATR SA THEMOONDANCER, and U.S. Reserve National Champion Mare JTH LF TRIUMPHANT STAR.

THEE DESPERADO has also sired international champions including World Champion Stallion BJ THEE MUSTAFA and National Champions in the Middle East, South Africa and Europe. THEE DESPERADO sons who have sired National winners include BB THE RENEGADE (sire of three times Top Ten Hunt Pleasure GALI GEE WHIZ SMF and paternal grandsire of U.S. National Champion Mare AOTH OM EL SHADREAM), BOTSWANA (sire of Israeli Egyptian Event Reserve Champion Mare KENYA PG), THEE INFIDEL (sire of 2 Sport Horse National halter winners), THEE VERDICT HG (sire of Scandinavian Reserve Champion Colt FLM VERDI) and TRUE COLOURS (sire of World Champion Stallion ROYAL COLOURS).
