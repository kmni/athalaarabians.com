CANNETTE


CANNETTE combines the best of Egyptian, Polish and American breeding. She is a 2001 Egyptian Event Top Ten World Class Two Year Old Filly and her son A CANEN is a regional and Las Vegas World Cup halter winner. She is a full sister to halter AOTR reserve champion, hunt and western pleasure ribbon winner and four times Egyptian Event Top Ten Halter ROLEEYAH BEY.


CANNETTE?s sire RAISIN THEE CANE is a regional and Scottsdale halter winner who also has 4 Egyptian Event halter titles including winning his World Class in 2005. From a very limited number of get due to his early death he has sired class A halter champions and Egyptian Event winners. RAISIN THEE CANE is by THEE DESPERADO, sire of 12 North American National winners through 2010.
